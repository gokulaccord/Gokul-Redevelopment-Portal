Redevelopment Stages
Complete 8-stage process for Mumbai CHS redevelopment under MCS Act 1960 & MCGM DCR 2034
ℹ Gokul Accord is currently at Stage 1. All future stages depend on timely DA signing and MCGM approvals.
✓
Stage 2 — SGM resolution + RDC committee election
Special General Body Meeting held. ≥75% member consent obtained per MCS Act. RDC committee elected. Intimation to Registrar of Co-operative Societies filed.
Completed
2
Stage 2 — Structural / technical audit
Independent structural engineer appointed. Report submitted confirming building age and structural condition as basis for redevelopment under Bye-law 79A.
In progress
3
Stage 3 — PMC/Architect appointment · RFP issue · Developer selection
Project Management Consultant (PMC) appointed to assist society. RFP (Request for Proposal) issued to empanelled developers. Bids evaluated on: FSI/TDR utilisation, additional carpet area offered, corpus fund per sqft, transit rent per sqft per month, developer track record, MahaRERA compliance, financial strength.
RFP issued — 3 bids received — 2 developers shortlisted for site visit
Pending
4
Stage 4 — Development Agreement drafting & registration
DA drafted by society's solicitor. Key clauses: transit rent escalation, corpus fund deposit schedule, carpet area guarantee, bank guarantee from developer, construction timeline with penalty, force majeure. DA registered at Sub-Registrar Office (SRO). Power of Attorney executed by members.
Pending
5
Stage 5 — MCGM approvals (IOD → CC)
Architect submits building plans to MCGM. Intimation of Disapproval (IOD) issued. Plans sanctioned. Commencement Certificate (CC) obtained. NOC from BMC fire brigade, electricity, tree authority etc.
Pending
6
Stage 6 — Transit rent payment & member vacating
Developer begins transit rent payments per DA. Members vacate within agreed timeline. Hardship allowance if applicable. Security deposit returned by society. Personal belongings move-out facilitated.
Pending
7
Stage 7 — Demolition & construction
Old structure demolished (BMC permit required). New building construction begins. PMC monitors construction milestones monthly. RDC receives progress reports. Structural completion, slab casting, external work tracked.
Pending
8
Stage 8 — OC, possession & society reformation
Occupancy Certificate (OC) obtained from MCGM. Individual flats measured and handed over. New society registered under MCS Act. Share certificates reissued. Property card / 7/12 updated.
Pending
